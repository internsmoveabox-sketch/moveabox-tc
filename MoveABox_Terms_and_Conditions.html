7
Courier Services — Specific Terms
New

7.1 — Weight & Dimension Charges

All courier shipments are charged based on Chargeable Weight, which is the higher of:

  • Actual Weight (in kg), OR
  • Volumetric Weight, calculated as:
L × B × H  (in cm)  ÷  4500  = Volumetric Weight (kg) Length × Breadth × Height in centimetres divided by 4500
Outstation Pickups: If a shipment is picked up from a city other than Bengaluru and a weight discrepancy is found at the origin point, a revised invoice will be generated and communicated to the customer before dispatch. The customer must settle any additional amount before the shipment is processed.

7.2 — Delivery Attempts & Hold Policy

MoveABox will make up to two (2) delivery attempts to the consignee address provided at the time of booking.

  • If the recipient is unavailable on the first attempt, the shipment will be placed on hold for 2–3 business days.
  • A second delivery attempt will be made within this hold period.
  • If the shipment remains undeliverable after the hold period, it will be processed as an RTO (Return to Origin).

7.3 — Return to Origin (RTO)

  • RTO charges will be applicable and must be borne by the sender/customer.
  • RTO charges are calculated based on the prevailing rate for the shipment route and weight.
  • No refund of the original delivery charges will be made in case of RTO.
  • MoveABox will notify the customer upon RTO initiation with an expected return timeline.

7.4 — Proof of Delivery (POD)

MoveABox follows a mandatory POD process for all courier deliveries to ensure secure and verified handovers:

  • Delivery completed upon successful OTP verification (sent to the registered mobile number of the recipient), OR
  • Presentation of a valid Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving Licence) of the recipient at the delivery address.
  • If neither is available, delivery will not be completed and the shipment will be held as per Section 7.2.
  • MoveABox reserves the right to capture a photo or digital record of the delivery confirmation.
